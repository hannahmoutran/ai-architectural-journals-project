Building and Construction Department

Charleston, S. C.—J. P. Gaillard will erect $5,000 frame residence; two stories and basement; tin roof; yellow pine floors; electric lights; plans and construction by owner.

Cordele, Ga.—Architect E. P. Dennis, American National Bank Bldg., Macon, Ga., is preparing plans for $3,000 residence for D. B. Brown.

Corinth, Miss.—Bungalow will be received by J. O. Kendrick.

Edgefield, S. C.—Architect G. Lloyd Preacher, Augusta, Ga., is preparing plans for $6,000 residence for R. F. Hill.

Jackson, Miss.—Architect N. W. Overstreet has prepared plans for $3,000 residence for T. B. Cabelly; two stories; frame.

Louisville, Ga.—Judge Milton Barwick will erect $7,000 residence. G. Lloyd Preacher, Augusta, Ga., architect.

Miami, Fla.—John H. Eastwood, manufacturer of Bellville, N. Y., will erect residence here this summer; plans for residence, which will cost $45,000, are now being prepared; Mr. Eastwood will be represented in the construction of his home by Phillips & Wainwright, real estate firm of Miami.

Pembroke, Ga.—Residence will be erected by Rev. V. P. Bowers; plans are being prepared.

St. Augustine, Fla.—Plans are being prepared by Architect Fred A. Hendrick, this city, for residence to be erected here by Mason Evans, Youngstown, O., a winter resident of St. Augustine.

Thomson, Ga.—John Boyd will erect $20,000 residence; plans are now being prepared by Architect G. Lloyd Preacher, Augusta, Ga.

Asheville, N. C.—H. C. Hudson will erect 8-room residence; cost $5,000.

Richmond, Va.—College Apartment Corporation will erect 3-story brick apartment-house at Lombardy and Grace Sts.; cost $44,000.

Atlanta, Ga.—Albert Howell, Jr., Connally Bldg., will erect addition to Ponce de Leon Apartments, practically duplicate of present structure; 80x120 ft.; steel or reinforced concrete construction; concrete and tile floors; steam power heat; cost $40,000; architect or contractor not selected.

Jacksonville, Fla.—College Apartment Corporation will erect apartment-house at Lombardy and Grace Sts.; 3 stories; brick; Mansard tile and slag roof; steam heat; electric lights; cost $44,000.

Miami, Fla.—J. N. Chamberlain contemplates erecting 3 additional floors on Chamberlain Apartments.

Miami, Fla.—Frank Gallat will erect apartment-house; 3 stories; cost $30,000.

Miami, Fla.—F. M. Brown will erect apartment-house; 7 stories; cost $40,000; E. A. Nolan, Arch't., Miami.

Miami, Fla.—W. H. Harris will erect apartment-house in Fort Dallas Park.

West Palm Beach, Fla.—Dr. E. Frances Rowe will erect apartment-house.

Charlotte, N. C.—Mrs. Latta C. Johnston has plans by L. L. Hunter, Charlotte, to erect apartment-house; tapestry brick veneer; hot air heat; hot and cold water; cost $20,000; bids being estimated.

Jacksonville, Fla.—J. R. Hickman will erect apartment-house; 2 stories; 20 rooms; brick veneer; cost $17,500.

Dallas, Tex.—Miss Birdie Tucker has plans by C. W. Bulger & Son, Praetorian Bldg., Dallas, for apartment-house; 2 stories; 6 rooms; interior construction; roof; pine and oak floors; gas and electric fixtures; cost $8,000.

Dallas, Tex.—C. F. Winans will erect apartment-house; 36x46 ft.; frame; shingle roof; quarter-sawed pine floors; natural-gas heat; electric lights; cost $3500; plans and construction by owner.

El Paso, Tex.—Tom B. Newman will erect 2-story tenement; cost $800.

El Paso, Tex.—John Stratton will erect brick apartment-house; cost about $25,000.

Houston, Tex.—E. Houck will erect apartment-house; 3 stories; brick; cost $8000.

San Antonio, Tex.—Robert Duncan will erect 2-story apartment-house; cost $8000.

Norfolk, Va.—August Coster will erect 4-family brick flat; cost $3000; also storage shed, $500.

Jacksonville, Fla.—G. F. Snead will erect 2-story brick dwelling; cost $4400.

Plant City, Fla.—Mrs. Helen S. McGilligray, Chicago, is reported to erect residence.

St. Petersburg, Fla.—Misses Edith and Grace Hill will erect brick-veneer 8-room residence; bungalow type; interior finish of oak; coil hot-water system of heat; also erect brick-veneer garage; total cost $10,000; W. J. Aires in charge of construction.

Atlanta, Ga.—J. F. Evans will erect $3000 dwelling.

Atlanta, Ga.—J. S. and C. R. Collins will erect residence; cost $3250; day labor.

Greenville, S. C.—H. M. Roswell will erect $4000 residence.

Dallas, Tex.—Sue C. Lynch will erect 8-room 2-story frame residence; cost $3000.

Dallas, Tex.—W. L. Burnett will erect 10-room 2-story frame residence; cost $4000.

El Paso, Tex.—P. H. Hadfield will erect 4 bungalows; cost $10,000.

Ennis, Tex.—Sanguinet & Staats, Fort Worth, and M. C. Parker, Ennis, prepared plans for residence and garage; 2 stories; basement; 10 rooms; brick and tile walls; tile, asbestos shingle or asphalt roof; hardwood, pine and oak floors; hot-air heat; cost $15,000; bids opened Apr. 25. Address Mr. Parker.

Church Buildings.

Augusta, Ga.—Bids received for Sunday school addition Greene St., Presbyterian Church; two stories; brick; slate or tin roof; warm-air or gas-steam heat; cost $12,000; plans by Bleckley & Irvin, King Bldg.

Bainbridge, Ga.—First Baptist Church; new edifice; address Pastor.

Fort Payne, Ala.—Methodist Church; new building; cost $100,000; 12 class-rooms. Address the Pastor.

Fort Payne, Ala.—First Baptist Church plans erection of $10,000 edifice. The Pastor.

Greer, S. C.—Baptist Church will erect $3,000 building; contract will soon be let. The Pastor.

Heavener, Okla.—First Baptist Church, Rev. W. J. Naylor, Pastor, is reported having plans prepared by W. A. Rayfield & Co., P. O. Box 649, Birmingham, Ala., for building; 50 x 95 ft.; ordinary construction; slate roof; pine and oak interior; electric lights; cost $10,000; day labor; bids opened May 1. Address Architect.

Hillsdale, Okla.—Congregational Church will erect building; cost $6000. Address R. Pope.

Medina, Okla.—Presbyterian Church will erect building; 40x60 ft.; cost $15,000. Address The Pastor.

Gainesville, Tex.—Grand Avenue Baptist Church, R. H. Seabough, Pastor, 621 N. Clement St., will erect building.
