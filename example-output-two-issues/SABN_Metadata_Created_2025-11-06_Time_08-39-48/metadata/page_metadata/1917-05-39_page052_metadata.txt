SOUTHERN ARCHITECT AND BUILDING NEWS

WE'RE FURNACE PIPE SPECIALISTS

The making of pipe and fittings for warm air heating plants is our entire business. For years we have been devoting all of our time and study to this work.

That's why Meyer products are known from one end of the land to the other as the most dependable and economical—why we can save you time and money on your furnace installations—why pipe and fittings bought here cost less than the ones you make in your own shop.

Meyer's Nested Furnace Pipe

is but one of the many time and money saving products that our line puts at your disposal. We carry every conceivable style and size of pipe or fittings required when installing a modern heating plant.

Send for catalog, quotations on Nested Furnace Pipe and full particulars regarding the famous "Handy" line of double pipe and fittings.

F. MEYER & BROTHER CO.,
Peoria, Ill.
